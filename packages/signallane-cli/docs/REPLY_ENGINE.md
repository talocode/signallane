# Reply Engine

SignalLane v0.1.0 uses deterministic local templates first.

## Reply generation

`signallane reply` produces three options:

1. Short punchy reply
2. Thoughtful reply
3. Builder-story reply

## Improve

`signallane improve` reduces filler, trims length, and keeps the user's voice more human.

## Score

`signallane score` evaluates:

- clarity
- relevance
- originality
- human tone
- conversation fit
- risk of sounding like AI
